Welcome to the Trentham Archery Club

Although the full name of the club is Trentham Camp Archery Club it is generally known as the Trentham Archery Club.

The objective of the Club is to perpetuate, foster and promote the practice of archery as a pastime, recreation and sport in all its forms in accordance with the spirit and honourable traditions of the sport. The objective is achieved by the encouragement and coaching of its members in the practice of all forms of Archery.

If you would like to become involved in the sport, or have any questions please do not hesitate in contacting one of the members listed on the Contact Us page of this website.
